How engagements work

Most work begins with a brief discovery call. If there’s a fit, I scope a review and deliver a written summary with prioritized recommendations and next steps.

  1. Discovery: goals, constraints, and operational context
  2. Review: workflow walk-through, dependency mapping, failure scenarios
  3. Deliverable: risk exposure summary + prioritized mitigation plan

Who this is for

Teams where time and accuracy matter—live events, automated playout, multi-feed environments, and operations that cannot afford preventable outages.

  1. Broadcast operations & master control environments
  2. Streaming teams supporting live programming or sports
  3. Organizations scaling distribution without mature runbooks
  4. Teams exposed to single-point-of-failure staffing or systems

About

Led by Randy Anthony, a broadcast and streaming operations specialist with decades of hands-on experience in time-critical environments. In these systems, failure rarely announces itself—issues surface quietly, compound quickly, and only become visible when timing is already compromised.

The focus is practical reliability: clarifying workflows, strengthening system visibility, and reducing the points where small problems turn into operational disruption.

The result is steadier execution, faster recovery, and systems that behave more predictably under pressure.
