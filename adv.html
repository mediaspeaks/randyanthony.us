How engagements work

Most work begins with a brief discovery call. If there’s a fit, I scope a review and deliver a written summary with prioritized recommendations and next steps.

  1. Discovery: goals, constraints, and operational context
  2. Review: workflow walk-through, dependency mapping, failure scenarios
  3. Deliverable: risk exposure summary + prioritized mitigation plan
