What Time-Critical Operations Teach You About Prevention

Week 12 · Topic word: Prevention · Optional: Link to LinkedIn post

Most organizations measure response.

Reliable systems measure prevention.

Prevention is quiet work:

  • Reviewing assumptions before they drift
  • Stress-testing transitions
  • Auditing dependencies
  • Updating documentation before it becomes outdated

When prevention works, nothing happens.

There is no dramatic recovery.

No visible save.

No post-incident praise.

Prevention reduces heroics.

It increases stability.

Reliability is not reactive.

It is designed.
