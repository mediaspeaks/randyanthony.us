Topic Rotation — Part I

This Operational Architecture Series is built around a 13-week cycle focused on foundational system behavior in time-critical environments.

Reliability is not a single capability. It emerges from disciplined execution, clear ownership, structured handoffs, and reducing hidden failure points across transitions.

These principles are explored through real-world operational patterns in broadcast and streaming systems.

Part I establishes the foundation. Part II expands into visibility, perception, and real-time decision-making under pressure.
