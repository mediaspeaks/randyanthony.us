Bio

Randy Anthony works at the intersection of operations, reliability, and time-critical systems. His career spans decades in environments where timing is unforgiving, automation is complex, and problems must be solved before they escalate.

While much of his background includes radio, television, and digital media delivery, his core expertise is operational: monitoring complex workflows, coordinating human and automated systems, and maintaining execution under real-world pressure. He is known for calm judgment, systems thinking, and the ability to keep critical processes running cleanly and predictably.

Randy combines hands-on experience with practical use of modern tools—including AI—to streamline workflows, improve documentation, support troubleshooting, and assist decision-making. He uses AI as an operational assistant to reduce friction and cognitive load, while relying on experience and judgment to guide outcomes.

He is also the author of FM – The Fading Modulation of Terrestrial Radio, a reflective examination of the evolution of radio and media through the lens of long-term industry experience.

Outside of work, Randy enjoys live music, cooking at home, nostalgic cars, and spending time near the coast. He remains interested in roles and projects where reliability, continuity, and clear thinking matter—across industries and evolving technologies.
